Know Your Elected Officials

 

For WSAP to thrive as a community and to ensure we receive all county and state services or benefits to which we are entitled as citizens, you MUST know who your elected representatives are.  It also helps to contact them when you have questions or issues you would like them to support.  Cut and paste the list into a document you keep on your computer.  Keep the list handy; use it often; and, VOTE based upon the constituent services you may have received from each.
