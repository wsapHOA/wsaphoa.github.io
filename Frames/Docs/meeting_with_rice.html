Board meets with Councilmember Rice Re: Proper County Maintenance of Community Streets

 

Board President Gabe Chikes, and Property Manager Steve Leskowitz recently met with Councilmember Rice to discuss theCounty's apparent oversight of their responsibility to maintain street beds (milling and paving) on Mill House, Metz, Open Hearth, Smokehouse and Well House.

 

The Councilmember, having verified the oversight with MoCo DOT, provided assurances that he will do whatever he can to have each street milled and paved sometime during 2018 (between April and October).

 

As there is a significant bureaucratic process involved in making this happen, the WSAP Board will circulate a petition during the month of March to be signed by those who live on each street in order to demonstrate the level of concern within the community and the number of voters impacted.

 

We hope that when the petition comes around, each of you will be willing to sign it.  With this or any request we make to the County, there IS strength in numbers.
