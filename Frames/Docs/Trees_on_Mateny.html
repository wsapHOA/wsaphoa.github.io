County to Assume Ongoing Maintenance of Trees Lining Mateny Road

 

After several attempts, the WSAP Board secured an agreement with the MoCo DOT, Tree Maintenance Section to acknowledge and assure that from March 2018 onward, they will be solely responsible for the maintenance and/or replanting of all trees along that section of Mateny Road that runs through the WSAP community.  While this had been a cost borne by the HOA, it shall be no longer.

 

If you notice any problems with or have complaints about these trees (primarily Bradford Pears) you may use this link: MoCo DOT Tree Maintenance Section.
