Parking

If you were parked illegally in the Williamsburg Square / Ashton place community and your car was towed, here is the information you need:

Henry's Wrecker Service
8321 Beechcraft Ave, Gaithersburg
301-869-4800

You can also call this number if someone is parked in your reserved parking spot or a tow-away zone, and you want to have them towed.

Report a vehicle violating the parking rules. Need to report an abandoned vehicle, a vehicle with no tags or expired tags or a vehicle that is violating one of the WSAP parking rules? Report it to us by sending an email to carreport@wsaphoa.org. Be sure and include the address where the car is parked and describe the vehicle and nature of the problem. Your message will be forwarded to the WSAP Board and the management company to be followed up on.
