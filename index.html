Trash Pick-up

NOTICE

Trash Pickup change occured January 1, 2022.

J&J Inc Trash Service will be the new contractor.

Trash is picked up twice per week, Monday and Thursday. Recycling (County) will continue on Fridays.

NO trash service on Holidays - trash collection will be done on the NEXT scheduled day.

HOLIDAYS are : New Years Day, MLK Day, Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day.

If you need help disposing of a large item visit the Montgomery County Bulk Trash Pickup Web Site

Trash Service is for household common trash only NO BULK items, Contact J&J Trash to make special THURSDAY only bulk arrangements at your expense

Some items that should not be put out for trash include:

  • Appliances
  • Furniture: Sofas, computer desks, book stands, TVs and TV stands
  • Scrap Wood
  • Sinks, Toilets, Bath tubs or any other large plumbing parts
  • Dirt
  • Hazardous Material, tires, batteries, paints, fuels etc.

Also remember that trash cans and recycle bins should NOT be stored in front of the house.

Was your trash not picked up or have a question about something you can dispose of? For questions about your trash only, contact J&J Trash Service 301-898-2109.
