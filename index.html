Trash Pick-up

Trash is picked up twice per week, Tuesday and Friday. When there is a holiday, trash pickup will still follow the Tuesday/Friday schedule.

Please retrieve your trash cans by close of day and remember that trash cans and recycle bins should not be stored in front of the house.

Some items that you should not be put out for trash for regular trash collection include:

  • Appliances
  • Furniture: Sofas, computer desks, book stands, TVs and TV stands
  • Scrap Wood
  • Sinks, Toilets, Bath tubs or any other large plumbing parts
  • Dirt
  • Hazardous Material

Please do not increase our HOA fees by relying on the HOA to dispose of these items at everyones expense. For free disposal of such items please visit Montgomery County Scrap Metal Pickup for things like appliances, grills, mowers.. or Potomac Disposal Bulk Trash Pickup for things like furniture, toilets, beds..

If your trash not picked up contact Potomac Disposal.
